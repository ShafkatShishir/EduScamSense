# Research Questions

## RQ1

How accurately can lightweight machine-learning models classify Bangla and English text messages as phishing or legitimate?

## RQ2

To what extent are the developed Bangla–English cybersecurity scenarios considered correctly labelled, realistic, linguistically appropriate, culturally suitable and safe by independent reviewers?

## RQ3

How can phishing analysis, scenario-based learning and awareness of AI-enabled scams be integrated into a bilingual cybersecurity education platform for educators?

## RQ4

How usable, understandable and relevant is the EduScamSense prototype according to expert or participant evaluation?
